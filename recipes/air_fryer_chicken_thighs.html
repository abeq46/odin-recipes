Air Fryer Chicken Thighs

5 cooked delicious looking Air Fried Chicken Thighs

This recipe is simple yet delicious, 10 minutes of prep with a 20 minute cook time for a total of 30 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Olive oil
  • Spices and Seasonings

Steps

  1. Pat the thighs dry and brush the skins with olive oil
  2. Make the spice blend and sprinkle it all over the thighs
  3. Cook in the preheated air fryer until the skin is brown and the jucies run clear

Serving Instructions

Boneless chicken thighs should be fully cooked after about 18 minutes in an air fryer preheated to 400 degrees F. Make sure you chek the internal temperature before you serve. The thermometer should read 165 at the center
