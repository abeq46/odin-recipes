Ham and Potato Soup

This recipe is warm and cozy, 20 minutes of prep with a 20 minute cook time for a total of 40 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • Onions, celery, potatoes
  • Water
  • Cooked Ham
  • Chicken bouillon granules, Salt, Black pepper
  • Butter and Flour
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil the ham and veggies until tender, then season.
  2. Make a roux in a saucepan. Whisk in the milk.
  3. Pour the milk mixture into the pot and stir until warm.

Serving Instructions

Feel free to add any or all of shredded Cheddar cheese, crumbled bacon, green onions, and chives. The dish paris well with crusty bread and a fresh side salad.
